# Guide

So, you want to learn a language to be able to speak to people all across the world. You've come to the right place! These are simple lessons designed for the layman, so no linguistics jargon here.

Looking for a quick look-up on grammar, or wanna see the technical details? See our [reference](../reference.md) page.

### 1. [Sounds and spelling](./1_pronunciation.md)

Learn how to read and write Baslamo. Here, we'll take a look at the letters of Baslamo and how to pronounce them.

### 2. [How to make a sentence](./2_sentences.md)

Learn how to make basic sentences. We'll learn the usage of _li_ and _e_, how to form statements and questions, and introduce a few core words to play around with.

### 3. [Making new words](./3_derivation.md)

Learn how to create new words using compounds and _kesekmolas_. We'll create words for tools and occupations, make causing and becoming words, and learn the rules for compound words.

### 4. [Incomplete, complete, and future actions](./4_tense_and_aspect.md)

Learn how to talk about when actions happen. We'll take another look at _li_, introduce the particles _at_ and _sa_, and learn how English tenses match to Baslamo's.

### 5. [Understanding _doskamolas_](./5_correlatives.md)

Learn how to use _doskamolas_, a collection of very useful words. Here we'll learn how to talk about "this", "every", "none", and "some", and how to ask questions like "how much?" and "who?".

### 6. [How to make a sentence, but harder](./6_hard_sentences.md)

Learn how to make more complex sentences. We'll learn about modal verbs, noun phrases, and introduce prepositional verbs.

### 7. [Names of people and places](./7_proper_nouns.md)

Learn how to fit names into Baslamo's sound system. We'll learn some Baslamo words that come from English, what sounds become which letters, and finish up by reading a few conversations.

### 8. [Introduce yourself](./8_introduction_exercise.md)

Make an introduction in Baslamo. This'll include your name, where you're from, what you speak, and your hobbies. Congratulations on making it this far!
